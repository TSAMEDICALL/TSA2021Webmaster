About the Service!

Our company was founded on the desire to make medical care easier and more accessible than ever before. And, as the past year has proven, that is a higher priority than ever before. In the last 18 months, we have served over five million customers from the comfort of their home.

Back in 2014, Lovemore Yamin, Wallace Hervey, and Angelia Beatrice found the company with the ambition to bring medical care to a virtual environment. Now with a small, dedicated team of over 3,000 members, the company has expanded to meet the needs of citizens around the country. Our goal is clear: to bring medical attention to you virtually as easily as possible. With our list of specialists, we guarantee the right doctor or specialist will be available for your needs with no extra cost from us.
